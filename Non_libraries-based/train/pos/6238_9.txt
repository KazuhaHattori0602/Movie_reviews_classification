uncompromising look at a suburb in  st century vienna mixing the stories of six groups of characters by former documentary maker u seidl is a provocative  minimalistic and intense piece of observation cinema   after the world wide spread of big brother reality shows  hundstage takes modern voyeurism to an unsettling  profound level  hard to like but unignorable piece of european art cinema might seem cruel and seedy  yet manages to convey the nihilistic alienated feeling of modern society in a praiseworthy manner   a must for lovers of world cinema
